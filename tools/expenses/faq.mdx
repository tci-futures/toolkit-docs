## General Questions

### Who do I contact for help with the Expenses App?

For help with the Expenses App, please contact the Accounts Team for help with the financial aspects, or speak to Mike Rudge about any technical difficulties.

## Troubleshooting

### How do I cancel or delete an expense I have submitted?

Once an expense has been submitted to your manager for approval, you cannot delete or cancel it yourself. If you need to make changes, please contact your manager or the Accounts Team for assistance.
